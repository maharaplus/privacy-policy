Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we protect your information.

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mahara+ center ("we," "our," or "the Service Provider") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of the Mahara+ mobile application (the "Application").

The Application is an educational platform designed for a tutoring and learning. It facilitates communication and learning management between administrators, teachers, students, and parents. User accounts are created exclusively by the center's administrator.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1

Information We Collect

a) User-Provided Information

Since user registration is managed by the center administrator, the personal information in your account (such as your name, email address, role (student, parent, teacher), age, assigned subjects, and class groups) is provided to us by your educational institution. You may provide additional information, such as a profile picture, or a phone number within the Application.

We use this information to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Facilitate communication between users (e.g., via the in-app chat).
  • Assign and manage educational content, subjects, and groups.
  • Provide important notices and updates related to the Application and your account.

b) Automatically Collected Information

To improve the Application and ensure its stability, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Type of mobile device, unique device identifiers, operating system, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Application, such as feature interaction and session length.
  • Performance Data: Diagnostic information related to Application crashes and performance.
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate the Application and provide its core features, including chat, file sharing, and user management.
  • To Improve the Application: To analyze usage trends and performance, helping us enhance user experience and develop new features.
  • To Ensure Security: To monitor and protect the security of our Application and users.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you service-related notices (e.g., account activity, policy changes).
3

Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies governing data use. We utilize these services for analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, and file management.

4

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your name, role, and profile picture may be visible to other users within the Application as required for its educational purpose (e.g., teachers can see their students, students in a group can see each other).
  • With Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our service (e.g., Firebase, Cloudinary), subject to strict data protection agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.
5

Data Retention

We will retain User Provided Data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you must contact your center administrator. We will retain Automatically Collected information for internal analysis purposes, typically for a shorter period.

6

Children's Privacy

The Application serves students, including children under the age of 13. However, all user accounts are created and managed by a center administrator (such as a school or tutoring center). We rely on these institutions to obtain the necessary parental consents required by law for children under 13.

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at mahara.formation@gmail.com, and we will work with the relevant administrator to address the issue.

7

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8

Your Rights and Choices

You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Application. You may also request to access, correct, or update your personal information by contacting your center administrator.

9

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
